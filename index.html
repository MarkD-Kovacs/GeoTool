GeoTool

This app combines the functionality of a ruler, protractor and compass into one tool, called GeoTool (the blue dashed line).

Just like real-world tools, it has dual purpose: drawing and measuring. It delivers an intuitive experience by using bidirectional fields, which display information in real-time and can also be used to control the GeoTool.

Each input field for the GeoTool also has a built-in calculator. Type in any valid math expression and press Enter to get the result and update the GeoTool instantly. The calculators support the following symbols and functions:

  • "pi" and "e" as mathematical constants
  • "^" for exponentiation: x^y
  • "sqrt" function for square roots: sqrt(x)
  • "log" function: log(x, base)

Other features:

  • Use the magnetic property to snap objects to other objects or to grid intersections
  • Double-click anywhere to create a new point
  • Drag and drop objects to move them around
  • Drag from an empty spot to move the canvas
  • Zoom in or out by scrolling or pinching
  • Switch between measuring angles in degrees or radians
